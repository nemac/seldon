Layers

Seldon can display multiple layers of data color-coded and in a legend for your analysis.

Multi-map Views

Switch between different map views to see Terrain, Topography, Imagery, Relief, and more.

Print View

Don't worry about making it all fit onto a page. There's a print button to make your page look great!

Masking

Conceal and reveal by data type. Wetland only, or Forest only, etc.

Multigraph Integration

Integrate Multigraphs to display data over a time period.

Identity Tool

Click a location and find metadata about a particular point on the map!

URL Sharing

Need to share the exact same map you're viewing? Easily create a link to share with others.

Mobile Supported

Need to browse on a mobile device? No problem, the pages are responsive!
